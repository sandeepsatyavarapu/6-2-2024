Taj Hotels is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited,[1] headquartered in Mumbai, India. Incorporated by Jamsetji Tata in 1902,[2] the company is a part of the Tata Group. The company employed over 20,000 people in the year 2010.[3][4] The company has been ranked as the World’s Strongest Hotel Brand in 2022[5] and India’s Strongest Brand as per Brand Finance Hotels 50 Report 2022 and India 100 Report 2020,[6] 2022[7] and 2023.[8]
